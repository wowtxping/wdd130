Chengdu

Chengdu, the capital of Sichuan, China, is famous for its Sichuan cuisine and the Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding. It's a city where ancient traditions meet modern life, offering a dynamic mix of history, technology, and leisure. Visitors can explore vibrant streets, historical sites, and enjoy the city's laid-back atmosphere with teahouses and parks, making it a unique blend of the old and new.
